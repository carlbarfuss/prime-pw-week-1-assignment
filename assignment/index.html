My favorite quote - “He who fights with monsters might take care lest he thereby become a monster. And if you gaze for long into an abyss, the abyss gazes also into you.” - Friedrich Nietzsche. I feel that this quote really encapsulates the effect that our efforts and our environment have on us, both good and ill, and speaks to why we should be careful at the endeavors to which we strive, the company we keep, and the methods we employ.
